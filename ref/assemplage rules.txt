
Assemblage
Instruction Manual
Objective: Put together the largest ensemble of forms by strategically selecting cards from the table. Make sure you use all the cards you pick because cards outside a complete ensemble will cost you points. The player with more points when no cards remain in the table wins the game.
Components:
•	16 point cards – 1 point
•	11 line cards – 16 points
•	10 triangle cards – 14 points
•	11 square cards – 12 points
Setup:
1.	Shuffle all the cards
2.	Place the cards on the table facing up forming a 4 X 12 grid
Gameplay:
1.	A random player starts.
2.	On your turn: 
a. Take one card from the extreme of a row (you can’t take cards from the middle)
b. Place the card in front of you and start forming ensembles with the other cards you have
3.	Continue clockwise until all cards are taken.
Assambling rules: 
Each card has connection points on one or more of its sides. You can put a card on the side of another if the number of connection points on both matches.
In a complete ensemble, all the cards have all their points connected with another card.
End of Game: The game ends when all cards are taken. Players count points following this rules:
•	Sum the points of all complete ensembles
•	Substract the points of all cards outside complete ensembles
•	Add 50 extra points to the player with the largest ensemble. If there is a tie, split the points between the players with the 
Winning: The player with the most points wins.
